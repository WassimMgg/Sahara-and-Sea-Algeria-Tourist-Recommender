About this project

A tourist recommendation system for Algeria.

Sahara & Sea recommends Algerian tourist attractions tailored to each visitor's taste. It demonstrates a complete recommender-system pipeline: collecting messy real-world data, cleaning it, comparing several algorithms, and serving the best one through a live web application.

How it works

Friends rated 15 attractions, producing a messy ratings file (inconsistent country names, mixed rating formats, missing values, duplicate entries). A cleaning step normalises all of this into a tidy dataset. Four collaborative-filtering models are then evaluated with cross-validation, and the best personalised model is saved and loaded by the backend.

When you rate a place, your ratings are stored and fed into the model, which immediately recomputes your recommendations — no retraining needed.

Technology

  • Backend — Django, with a SQLite database seeded from the CSV files.
  • Model — collaborative filtering written from scratch in Python (NumPy).
  • Frontend — HTML, CSS and JavaScript, responsive and animated.

The catalogue

From the Casbah of Algiers and the Roman ruins of Timgad and Djemila, to the Tassili n'Ajjer plateau and the Hoggar mountains deep in the Sahara — the places span every region of Algeria.
